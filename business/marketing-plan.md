# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
Scope Shield is the essential tool for open source developers and maintainers to define and manage project scope effectively, preventing scope creep and burnout.

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**
1. Scope Shield empowers open source projects to stay on track by providing clear scope management tools, reducing developer fatigue.
2. With Scope Shield, open source maintainers can effortlessly define project boundaries, ensuring sustainable development without burnout.
3. Scope Shield is the go-to solution for open source teams seeking to maintain focus and clarity in their projects, avoiding the pitfalls of scope creep.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Open Source Maintainer**
   - **Daily Job:** Manages a popular open source library, coordinates contributions, and handles community feedback.
   - **Budget:** $500/month for tools to enhance project management and community engagement.

2. **Persona 2: Jamie the Independent Developer**
   - **Daily Job:** Works on multiple open source projects, juggles contributions, and struggles with prioritizing tasks.
   - **Budget:** $300/month for tools that help streamline project management and reduce overwhelm.

3. **Persona 3: Taylor the Project Lead**
   - **Daily Job:** Oversees a team of developers on an open source project, responsible for project timelines and deliverables.
   - **Budget:** $1,000/month for comprehensive project management solutions that integrate with existing workflows.
